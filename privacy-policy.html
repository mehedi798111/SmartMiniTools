Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

Our Privacy Commitment

At SmartMiniTools, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we handle your information when you use our tools and services.

Information We Don't Collect

We want to be clear: we do not collect, store, or process any personal information on our servers. All tools run locally in your browser.

How Our Tools Work

All processing happens entirely in your web browser. When you use our tools:

  • Your text, passwords, or data never leave your device
  • No information is sent to our servers
  • No cookies or trackers are used
  • No data is stored or logged

Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to our website or other websites. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at their privacy policy page.

Children's Privacy

Our services are safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date.
